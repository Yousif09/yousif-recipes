Biryani

Description:

Biryani (either chicken or beef) is a hearty dish that is packed with rice, a lot of vegetables, and the meat of your choice. It is usually spicy and topped off with some yogurt on the top.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Meat of your choice
  • Peas,Carrots, and onions
  • Yogurt

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Mix the chicken/lamb/beef inside with all of the vegetables
  3. Serve with yogurt on the top
  4. Enjoy :)
