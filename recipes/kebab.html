Kebabs

Description:

Kebabs are an arab dish and they are primarily made with beef,lamb and chicken. They are then grilled to perfection. The toppings are usually just salt and some tomatoes.

Ingredients:

  • Beef/Lamb/Chicken
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Press the meat onto a skewer
  2. Drizzle some salt onto it
  3. Grill to perfection
