Dolmas

Description:

Dolma is a stuffed dish largely associated with the Ottoman cuisine. It includes vegetables and leaves, primarily grape leaves. Sometimes rice, meats, and tomatoes are stuffed inside topped off with some olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • Grape Leaves
  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Meat: Lamb or Beef
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Put the grape leaves down and pack it with your neccessities
  2. With the carefully wrapped dolmas put it in a pan to boil
  3. Wait 1-2 hours and then when they are soft to the touch they are ready to eat
